Sleep Medicine Consultants we are here for you!

Clinical Services

We treat all variety of sleep disorders: sleep apnea, insomnia, hypersomnia [narcolepsy, idiopathic hypersomnia], parasomnia, restless leg syndrome, circadian rhythm disorders, jet lag, etc... We offer virtual visits from home/work with extended hours and on weekends for your convenience.

Testing

We offer both polysomnography (PSG) in lab sleep testing and an home sleep apnea testing (HSAT).

Treatment Options

We guide your personalized treatment plan offering access to Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for Insomnia, CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Treatment), referrals for dental devices and efficacy retesting, positional and/or behavioral therapy, surgery Consultants with follow-up care, and medication management (when needed).
